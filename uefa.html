TEAMS(history)

Fc Barcelona


FC Barcelona, known as "Blaugrana" for its blue and deep red colors, is one of the most successful football clubs globally. Founded in 1899, the club has a rich history of domestic and international achievements. Notable periods include the "Dream Team".


Liverpool

Liverpool Football Club, established in 1892, is one of the most successful and storied football clubs in the world. Based in Liverpool, England, the club has a rich history of domestic and international achievements. Domestically, Liverpool has won a record-equalling 20 English league titles.


Bayern


FC Bayern Munich, founded in 1900, stands as Germany's most successful football club, boasting a record 34 Bundesliga titles and 20 DFB-Pokal victories. Internationally, Bayern has secured six UEFA Champions League trophies. They achieved a historic treble and a sextuple in 2020, making them one of only two clubs to achieve this feat.
